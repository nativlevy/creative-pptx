# Leave a Mark - Case Studies

## Case Study 1: TechStart SaaS Launch

### Client
TechStart - B2B SaaS platform for project management

### Challenge
TechStart needed to launch their new product in a crowded market dominated by established players. They had a superior product but limited brand awareness and marketing budget.

### Solution
We developed a comprehensive go-to-market strategy:
- **Brand Positioning**: Positioned TechStart as "The Project Management Tool Built for Growing Teams"
- **Visual Identity**: Created a fresh, modern brand that stood out from corporate competitors
- **Content Strategy**: Developed thought leadership content targeting their ideal customer profile
- **Launch Campaign**: Multi-channel campaign including LinkedIn ads, content marketing, and PR

### Results
- 300% increase in website traffic in first 3 months
- 150 qualified leads generated from launch campaign
- 45% conversion rate on demo requests
- Featured in 5 industry publications

## Case Study 2: HealthFirst Rebrand

### Client
HealthFirst - Regional healthcare network

### Challenge
HealthFirst's brand felt outdated and failed to convey their modern approach to patient care. They needed a complete brand refresh to attract both patients and top medical talent.

### Solution
- **Brand Strategy**: Developed new positioning around "Healthcare That Cares"
- **Visual Identity**: Complete redesign including logo, color palette, and photography style
- **Brand Guidelines**: Comprehensive 100+ page brand book
- **Implementation**: Rolled out across 15 facilities, digital properties, and all marketing materials

### Results
- Patient satisfaction scores increased 25%
- 40% improvement in brand perception surveys
- Recruitment applications up 60%
- Successfully unified brand across all locations
